Cookie Policy

This policy describes the use of cookies on the website www.paystree.com, specifying the purposes and type of use of cookies, and the right of users to deny the use of cookies.

What are cookies and why are they used?

Cookies are tiny text files that a web browser (for example, Microsoft Edge, Internet Explorer, Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari, etc.) stores on a computer, tablet or web user's smartphone when the user visits the website. By using cookies, websites can store the web user’s individual settings. Thus, with the help of cookies, the website is able to store the user’s individual settings, recognize him or her and react accordingly. A web application can adapt actions to the needs and interests of the user by collecting and storing information about the user's preferences. The user can disable or restrict the use of cookies, but without cookies, it will be impossible to fully use all the functions of the website.

The keeper uses cookies to analyse visits to the website and improve the website in accordance with the user's needs. This information is used exclusively for statistical analysis purposes, and then data is deleted from the system.

Cookies help to provide the best website for the user. The cookie does not in any way provide the keeper access to the user's computer. The user has the ability to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change the settings of your web browser to reject cookies. This can prevent a user from fully using the website.

Cookies vary depending on the purpose and duration of storage. Over time, the used cookies can be updated and adapted to the needs of improving the quality of service delivery.

For what purposes do we use cookies

We use cookies to improve our website, namely, in order to:
- provide the functionality of the website, receive statistics on the flow of visitors to the website - the number of visitors, the time spent on the page, etc.
- authenticate the user and customize the functionality of the website for user's user habits - including language, search terms, previously viewed content;
- in case the user is our client to authenticate the client, offer the client individual services and offers.

What cookies do we use?

Technical cookies are designed for the quality functioning of the website and for the convenience of the user, so that the website can work without problems and the user can connect to his account, if he or she has any.

Functional cookies are designed to allow the user to easily visit a website - for example, remembering the preferred language of the website, the services of interest and other things that make the website faster and more convenient in the long term.

Analytical cookies are designed to give us an idea of ​​the activities of our users on our website, which allows us to find out the advantages and disadvantages of the website, as well as what needs to be changed in the content or technical solutions of the website. Analytical cookies allow you to collect information about the user's web browser, the number of clicks on the website, the website from which the user came to our website, the keywords entered, date and the time they were created. The information received is completely anonymous and not associated with a specific person. This information is used to study the market, improve the website, plan the campaign and analyse the activity of users.

Marketing cookies are designed to allow the user to receive personalized ads on our website and on other websites. Promotional or commercial cookies guarantee that the user in the Internet environment receives offers based on the interests of users and the actions performed.

How can a user deny the use of cookies?

On our website, you cannot use cookies by changing the settings of your web browser or by deleting cookies, but in some cases, some constraints of certain functions may appear while browsing our website.
If the user wants to delete all cookies stored on the computer, or stop using cookies, he can do this by deleting existing cookies and/or changing the privacy settings of the web browser to block cookies (this process is different from the web browser to the web browser).
